Grand Slam Tournaments

The four major tournaments in tennis are the Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon, and the US Open.

Each Grand Slam tournament is unique in its own way:

  • Australian Open - Played on hard courts
  • French Open - Played on clay courts
  • Wimbledon - Played on grass courts
  • US Open - Played on hard courts
